About Our Company

At MW Training and Productivity Consultants Ltd., we specialize in delivering customized training, strategic consultancy, and systems optimization to elevate organizational performance across Zimbabwe.

Our experienced team works with government bodies, private sector entities, and NGOs to unlock productivity potential, foster innovation, and build resilient institutions that drive national development.

Our Mission

To enhance operational efficiency and foster sustainable growth through tailored training and consultancy services that address the unique challenges of Zimbabwean organizations.

Our Vision

To be the leading force in transforming Zimbabwean organizations into efficient, productive, and sustainable entities that compete effectively in the global marketplace.

Willias Madzimure is a seasoned management and productivity consultant with over three decades of experience spanning industrial engineering, systems analysis, organizational development, and public sector reform. With a robust academic background, he has consistently delivered practical, data-driven solutions to enhance institutional efficiency and productivity.
